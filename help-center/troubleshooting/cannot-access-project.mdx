---
title: Cannot Access My Project
description: Steps to resolve project access problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Project not loading or missing
- "Access denied" error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your login">
    Make sure you are signed in with the correct account at `https://app.xeyla.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm permissions">
    Ask your project admin to verify your access rights in the Team settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try refreshing or opening xeyla in a private window.
  </Step>
</Steps>
