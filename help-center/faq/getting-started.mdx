## Creating Your First Project

1. Sign in at `https://app.xeyla.com`.
2. Click **New Project** on the dashboard.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click **Create**.
5. Start adding and organizing your docs.

Your new project will show up in your dashboard. Use the sidebar in xeyla to manage project settings and invite team members.
